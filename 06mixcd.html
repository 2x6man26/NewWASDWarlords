Making mix CDs has been is major part of Levi's life. They were born from necessity. Levi's car is old and does not have Bluetooth so he started all this by making Mix CD 1 on June 6th 2023. They have morphed and evolved into a time capsule and not only play fire music while Levi is driving about but also remind him of awesome things that has happened in his past. One is made once every two months. In the beginning the music was required to be before the 2000s but Levi's music taste has broadened and this is no longer required.
